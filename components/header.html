Shuai Fu

Shuai Fu is a second-year PhD student at the Australian Institute for Machine Learning (AIML), The University of Adelaide, advised by Prof. Qi Wu. His research focuses on generative AI, particularly on factual-consistent image generation in generative models. Before starting his PhD, he worked as a research assistant at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) from 2022 to 2024, supervised by Prof. Yu Zhang. His broader interests include machine learning, multi-modal learning, and generative AI.
